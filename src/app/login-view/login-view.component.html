Welcome to Spartan Connect. There's a wide range of possibilities available! From here, you can:

  • View your announcements: Click on USER PANEL. From here, you can select from a range of filters.
  • Create an announcement: Click on USER PANEL, and then click on CREATE AN ANNOUNCEMENT.
  • Edit your announcement: Click on USER PANEL, click on RECENT FEED, and then scroll down until you locate your announcement. From there, you can click EDIT and edit your announcement.
